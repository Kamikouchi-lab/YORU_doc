## How to use

1. Use the "calibration_projecter_cv2.py" script to calibrate the camera and projector.  


2. Copy "projecter_drawing_wing.py" into the trigger_plugins folder of the YORU repository.  


3. Launch YORU and select "projecter_drawing_wing" as the trigger plugin.
